Who outranks who?

This page details who outranks whom, and who must obey whom, and what happens if there's a contradiction.

The Chain of Command

Listed in order of highest ranking to lowest ranking.

Site Owner (currently Shentino)
The person in charge of the "Phantasmal Test" site, which you are currently reading.
Mud Administrator
Each mud's top adminstrator. If a mud does not have an administrator, then the Site Owner shall be considered mud administrator.
Mud staff
Any staff of the mud in question. Further details are available with each mud. It may vary from time to time depending on the needs of the mud, as well as the qualifications of any applicants and even the theme of the mud itself.

Orders

An order is any directive, rule, or command issued by a staff member.

Command responsibility

Any order shall be considered to have been obeyed by whoever issued it. This applies regardless of its validity.

Conflicts

In event of conflict between two orders, the one of higher rank shall prevail. Should there be a tie in rank, then the order which was last issued shall prevail.

Any order issued in contradiction to an order of higher rank is null and void.
