The Big Six of Rules

These six rules apply on all three test muds. Follow them or suffer the consequences.

These rules apply to EVERYONE, even senior staff.

1. Obey the Law

There will be no sexual harassment, copyright infringement, fraud, theft, or other such nefarious activities that violate criminal law at either the federal, state, or local level of any applicable jurisdiction.

For sure, US federal law applies. I don't yet know where the server is physically located, so I'm not sure which state's laws apply. For now, use common sense.

2. Obey the Staff

There are a million things that can disrupt the MUDs that cannot possibly be listed explicity in any sort of rules. Therefore, staff have been entrusted with the duties of keeping order and harmony in the MUDs.

Details on the particulars of rank, precedence in the event of conflict, and so on can be found here.

3. Be Clean

Foul language, lewdness, profanity, and the like are not welcome. The MUDs are meant for public testing, not private perversion. Besides, some kid may stumble in, and we don't want to give any parents cause for alarm, do we?

4. Be Nice

A healthy attitude is a must when on our systems. Don't be an arsehole, no matter how bad RL gets. And do bear in mind that there are REAL PEOPLE connecting (well, they'd BETTER be real ;)). In short, "Remember the human".

5. Be Honest

Nothing screws up the "system" like people lying, deceiving, using false information, or otherwise being conniving or dishonest. Trust is the glue that holds the entire system together, and breaking it cannot be tolerated.

This includes, but is not limited to, using false information during registration, fabricating evidence in a harassment case, and the like. Just be truthful.

This also includes slyly botting or macroing. Botting and macroing, which are generally frowned upon, are especially forbidden if done secretly. It is effectively a breach of your claim that you are entirely human, a claim that you will make when you pass the captcha.

6. No Multiplay

The usage of more than one account per person, or the usage of an account by more than one person, is strictly prohibited. Keeping people accountable for their actions (either good or bad) requires clear association between an account and a person.

Any person who shall use more than one account, or who shall knowingly and willfully permit any other person to use any of their accounts with anyone else, shall be guilty of multiplay. Any account whose user is guilty of multiplay will be terminated.

Some accounts may be for purposes rather than people. These exceptions will be noted in advance.
