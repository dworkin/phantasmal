The Big Six of Rules

These six rules apply on all muds. Follow them or suffer the consequences.

These rules apply to EVERYONE, even senior staff.

1. Obey the Law

There will be no sexual harassment, copyright infringement, fraud, theft, or other such nefarious activities that violate criminal law at either the federal, state, or local level of any applicable jurisdiction.

The juristiction in which the server is located is Edmonton, Alberta, Canada.

Anyone found violating any sort of criminal statute that applies to the server shall be immediately and permanently banned.

2. Obey the Staff

There are a million things that can disrupt the MUDs that cannot possibly be listed explicity in any sort of rules. Therefore, staff have been entrusted with the duties of keeping order and harmony in the MUDs.

Details on the particulars of rank, precedence in the event of conflict, and so on can be found here.

3. Be Clean

Foul language, lewdness, profanity, and the like are not welcome. The MUDs are meant for public testing, not private perversion. Besides, some kid may stumble in, and we don't want to give any parents cause for alarm, do we?

4. Be Nice

A healthy attitude is a must when on our systems. Don't be a jerk, no matter how bad RL gets. And do bear in mind that there are REAL PEOPLE connecting (well, they'd BETTER be real ;)). In short, "Remember the human".

5. Be Honest

Nothing screws up the "system" like people lying, deceiving, using false information, or otherwise being conniving or dishonest. Trust is the glue that holds the entire system together, and breaking it cannot be tolerated.

6. No Multiplay

The usage of more than one account per person, or the usage of an account by more than one person, is strictly prohibited.
