Policies, Rules, and Regulations

Since this site is for actual, live, testing, and not just looking at, there will be some rules for usage of the test site. Each MUD may itself have its own policies. If so, they will be visible.

The Big Six, a set of six rules that apply globally, should be first reference. They apply to everyone, from the lowliest of guests to the loftiest of senior staff and perhaps even beyond.
