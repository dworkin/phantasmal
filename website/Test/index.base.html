Test MUDs

(Coming soon...)

Phantasmal, as a mudlib, should be generic and extensible enough to support different genres of MUD. So, courtesy of necronomicorp.com, three test MUDs will be hosted, and this should help debug things.

Please visit the MUD index here for more information on the test servers and their operational policies.

Design documents for each mud can be found at the following locations:

  • Seas of Night, a ghostly adventure on the high seas.
  • Innsmouth High, a pretend high school set in the Lovecraft genre.
  • Light v. Dark, a power balance game.
