MUD Design material tailored for Innsmouth and Phantasmal

  • Articles on MUD Design, culled from Skotos and elsewhere.
  • Procedural World Generation, mostly summarized from MUD-Dev, and especially from J.C. Lawrence's posts to that list
  • Various mostly-original speculations on Object Grouping and its relatives.
  • Speculations on Class Heirarchy and how it affects other aspects of the MUD.
  • Speculations on Visibility in the MUD, including details on perception, object grouping and event grouping.
  • Actions and Events, and how Phantasmal might deal with them.
  • Ideas about Procedural Generation of Systems (like towns, populations, countries) in a MUD.
