Innsmouth MUD

Innsmouth is a decaying Massachusetts town that hides a terrible secret. Like a snowball becoming an avalanche, like a lie that hides itself in larger lies, so has the Shadow Over Innsmouth has bred new terrors. The Shadow waits for you to explore this tiny, decrepit and terrified New England town.

Innsmouth MUD is inspired by the story "The Shadow Over Innsmouth" by H. P. Lovecraft, and other short stories. It was built as a test MUD for the Phantasmal MUD Library.

In its current condition, it isn't meant to be a full-on Lovecraft experience in MUD form. Rather, it's a set of interesting vignettes meant to showcase Phantasmal's abilities. I hope to produce something worthy of the gentleman from Providence eventually, but that's a long-term goal.

You can find some of Lovecraft's work on the web. My understanding is that his stuff is no longer under copyright due to failure to renew on the part of Arkham Press (and on his own behalf). The Gizmology.net Lovecraft Archive seems to be down, or I'd link to that one, and the note on copyrights to the stories. You could check the Wayback Machine for them, I guess.
