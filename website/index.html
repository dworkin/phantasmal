The Phantasmal MUDLib for DGD

Phantasmal: pertaining to or of the nature of a phantasm

Phantasm: 1. an apparition or specter. 2. a creation of the imagination or fancy. 3. an illusive likeness of something. 4. a mental image or representation of a real object.

The Phantasmal MUDLib is a DGD MUDLib under active development. It's the best available MUDLib for DGD if you want to build most of your game for yourself, or if you want something unlike a traditional MUD. It is hosted on SourceForge. Current and older releases can be downloaded there.

Phantasmal is my personal attempt to learn DGD, and to put together a MUD server implementing certain things I think are cool. It isn't an attempt at a full-scale professional server. It certainly isn't an attempt at anything like the current crop of Diku derivatives. At the moment Phantasmal focuses on features that no other MUDLib provides, though it skimps on some of the old standbys. For instance, it has an excellent help system, recompile-in-place and some interesting input and output systems like UNQ and localization, but it has no combat system at all.

Phantasmal's design is heavily influenced by Skotos, and more specifically by certain of their articles.
